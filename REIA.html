NOAA Fisheries Integrated Toolbox

Tool: Regional Economic Impact Analysis for North Pacific Fisheries (REIA)

Authors

C.K. Seung and S. Miller.

Description

The Regional Impact Analysis web application was developed to assist economists and social scientists working with Alaska fisheries in estimating the economic impacts of changes in fishery policies. This web-based application runs a 10 region Multi-Regional Social Accounting Matrix (10MRSAM) model.
